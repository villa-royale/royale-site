Villa Frontage

A villa that presents an air of refined elegance fit for royalty, this palatial villa unfolds over 800 square meters of light-filled lustrous and textured spaces that frame startling, take-your-breath-away views. The villa is situated on an impressive private plot of impeccable landscaped gardens. The spectacular tree-lined driveway and regal, sweeping staircase of marble and bespoke balustrade set the tone for this breath-taking villa. Wrap around balconies to both the ground and upper floors completes the fairy-tale picture.

Entrance

Upon entering the villa, you are immediately greeted by a stunning, gold imperial staircase. Taking pride position in the center of the villa, it provides a spectacular centerpiece to the reception area and gallery upstairs. Lofty ceilings, crystal chandeliers and polished marble flooring create distinct elegance and grandeur in this masterpiece property.

Lounging

The spacious open plan and light filled living area features two very distinct and beautifully furnished lounging areas. Each perfectly designed space creating the opportunity for relaxation, conversation and entertainment. Adjacent to the formal living room is a TV with UK and SKY TV channels and Wi-Fi connectivity are available for your entertainment.

Dining Area

A great space for formal dining with a grand polished table and beautiful designer high-back leather chairs to seat 8. Enjoy the elegant ambiance and splendid grandeur of Villa Royale while you dine.Wonderful unobstructed views can be enjoyed from here as large patio doors frame the impeccable gardens.

A more informal dining option is also available and sited between the two kitchen areas, with a large glass topped decorative wooden table and 8 comfortable leather chairs.

Kitchen

There are two large kitchens in this villa. A working kitchen for food p[reparation, fully equipped with the latest appliances and one decorative kitchen ideal for catered events, entertaining and serving. Both boast black granite worktops with quality furnishings with all the added comforts you would expect, including a Nespresso Coffee machine. There is a window to the front aspect with sea and garden views and a large patio door leading directly to the front veranda for easy al fresco access. A large breakfast table with seating for an additional four people.

Gardens

Spectacular 8000 sqm gardens with traditional olive and avocado trees, inset palms and local cypress trees. A picturesque garden with immaculate lawns and decorative stone borders separated by miniature walling and beautiful flower beds that add a splash of colour and wonderful aroma. This is a truly amazing venue for any special occasion. The ideal romantic and elegant wedding venue.

Outdoor Living

Dip your toe into the sparkling waters of this fabulous 10m x 5m private pool with Jacuzzi set against the stunning backdrop of the surrounding Peyia mountains. Enjoying a cold beverage or perhaps just succumb to the allure of the daybeds which beckon you to an afternoon of reading, relaxing and sunbathing. At lunch, dine al fresco after cooking up a storm in the fully equipped outdoor sun pavilion and in the evening, relax back into the family size Hot Tub. The choices are endless, and none will disappoint. Villa Royal offers the ultimate in luxury Cyprus villa living.
