Welcome to Villa Royale

With the superb design and grandiosity of a 5 star hotel, yet the privacy of a home-away-from-home, this spectacular 7 bedroom villa, complete with private pool, jacuzzi and sun pavilion is the perfect destination for an indulgent getaway or dream wedding. Set within 8,000 square-meters of secured private land in the beautiful hillside village of Peyia and only minutes from the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea, Villa Royale is a magnificent base for exploring the Paphos region.
