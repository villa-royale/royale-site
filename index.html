Villa Royale

With the superb design and grandiosity of a 5 star hotel, yet the privacy of a home-away-from-home, this spectacular 7 bedroom villa, complete with private pool, jacuzzi and sun pavilion is the perfect destination for an indulgent getaway or dream wedding.

A villa that presents an air of refined elegance fit for royalty, this palatial villa unfolds over 800 square meters of light-filled lustrous and textured spaces that frame startling, take-your-breath-away views.
